Instructions

Once signed in successfully, you are presented with this interface.

To record a withdrawal or deposit, simply click on the record/edit withdraw button or the record/edit deposit button.

To hide these inputs, simply click on the hide withdraw information button or the hide deposit information button.

To submit these inputs, simply click on the respective submit button.

Once edits have been submitted, they can be edited by clicking the respective 'edit' button for the transaction.

Once the edit button has been clicked, the inputs will be automatically filled to their expected values. To hide the inputs again, click the respective 'hide edit' button for the transaction.

To delete a transaction, click the respective 'delete' button for the transaction.

To search or filter the transactions, simply click on the 'search' button or 'filter' button.

Once you click one of the two buttons, you should be presented with these inputs.

The search input should automatically update. To apply the entered filters, click on the 'filter' button. To clear the filters, click the 'clear' button.

To generate a summary about the transactions, click the 'generate monthly summary' button or 'generate weekly summary' button.

You will be presented with this summary.

To hide this summary, simply click the 'hide summary' button.

To email the info, click on the 'forward to email' button. A message indicating the outcome of the email will be displayed.
